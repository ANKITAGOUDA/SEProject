Education Specialist

Position Description

As an Education Specialist, you are responsible for developing curriculum materials, providing instructional support, and implementing educational programs. You work directly with teachers and students to enhance learning outcomes and support educational initiatives.

Core Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement curriculum materials and learning resources
  • Provide instructional support to teachers and educational staff
  • Evaluate and select appropriate educational technologies
  • Assess student learning outcomes and program effectiveness
  • Collaborate with teachers to enhance classroom instruction
  • Stay current with educational research and best practices
